**vnodeGets**  
*Type:* Counter  
Number of vnode-level GETs in past minute

**vnodePuts**  
*Type:* Counter  
Number of vnode-level PUTs in past minute

**nodeGets**  
*Type:* Counter  
Number of GETs in past minute

**nodePuts**  
*Type:* Counter  
Number of PUTs in past minute
